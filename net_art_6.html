Exhibition

UBERMORGEN shows the totality of all original documents from the legal proceedings, a total of 700kg worth of court documents, injunctions and such, in exhibitions worldwide.

These documents are refered to as forginals, a cheeky combination of the words forged and original. For UBERMORGEN this combination of fact and fiction shows the expansion of one's perception of concepts such as RIGHTS, DEMOCRACY and GLOBAL COMMUNICATION.

Vote-Auction has been exhibited in this manner in places ranging from the Lentos Kunstmuseum in Linz, Austria, to the Eyebeam here in New York City.
