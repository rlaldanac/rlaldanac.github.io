Related Projects

As a response to the somewhat controverial and illegal court orders sent from the US to Europe, UBERMORGEN created the Injunction generator.

This software module was designed to create an interface on which one can create their own cease-and-desist requests and other legal injunctions.

In the year 2004 UBERMORGEN released a project called *THE*AGENCY designed for manual elction recount. This became the 2nd part of what has now come to be known as the Election Trilogy.

The third part of the trilogy came in 2007 in the form of WAHLGELD.COM. This platform was designed as a space for vote-buying and selling within the context of Swiss parliamentary election. The Swiss election commission decreed that this project was non-commercial in motivation.
